**Decision Factory = the four-component machine your business uses to make decisions in a systematic, sustainable, scalable way.** Source: Liz's Apr 18 notebook.

OCDA Cockpit is the Decision Factory **made personal** — Observe · Choose · Decide · Act is the loop that makes the Factory compound rather than reset.

## The four canonical components

01. **Data pipeline** — Semi-automated process that gathers, cleans, integrates, and safeguards data. Lives in: SweetScan + Capture + inbound_signals.
02. **Algorithms** — Generates **predictions** of future states and actions of a business. Predictions, not reports. Lives in: KTIs + BizzyBots + Lenses.
03. **Experimentation platform** — Hypotheses tested. Confirms whether suggestions had their intended effect. Lives in: Workflows + Sessions.
04. **Infrastructure** — Embeds the process into software; connects internal + external users. What makes it compound. Lives in: the OS itself.

## Anti-EBITDA argument

Reports tell you what already happened. Decision Factory makes **forward-looking** predictions and tests them. Don't measure the past — engineer the next decision.
